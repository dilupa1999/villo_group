Our Journey

Company Achievements

Excellence in Multi-Industry Operations Across Kandy Region

Villo Group has successfully established itself as a trusted name across automotive, healthcare, and hospitality sectors in Kandy, Sri Lanka. Our commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction has earned us recognition as a leading diversified business group in the region.

2023 Automotive

Villo Auto Cars Achieves Top Customer Satisfaction Rating

Our automotive division reached a milestone with 95% customer satisfaction rating, reflecting our dedication to providing quality vehicles and exceptional after-sales service. We've expanded our service network and introduced advanced diagnostic equipment to better serve our customers.

2024 Healthcare

Villo Pharmacy Expands Community Health Programs

Successfully launched comprehensive health awareness programs reaching over 5,000 community members. Our pharmacy has become a trusted healthcare partner, offering specialized pharmaceutical consultations and maintaining the highest standards in medication dispensing and patient care.
