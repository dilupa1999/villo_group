Excellence Across Multiple Industries in the Kandy Region

Villo Group stands as a trusted leader in the automotive, healthcare, and hospitality sectors throughout Kandy, Sri Lanka. Our unwavering dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a recognized and respected diversified business group in the area.

2023 – Automotive Milestone

Villo Auto Cars proudly achieved a 95% customer satisfaction rating, highlighting our commitment to delivering quality vehicles and outstanding after-sales support. This year, we expanded our service network and invested in cutting-edge diagnostic technology to enhance customer care.
